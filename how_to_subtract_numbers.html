How to Subtract Numbers?

Here you will find instructions about using the subtraction feature of JPS Calculator App.

To successfully subtract numbers, you must know what is subtraction.
  1. Enter the First Number
  2. Choose the (-) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Choost the (=) button.
    The result from the subtraction will be displayed.

When you want to subtract 2 from 3, the equation will look like 3-2=1. First enter 3, then minus (-), later press 2, now press equal (=).

The result 1 will appear on the screen.
